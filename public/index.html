What do these numbers mean?

Hook score (0-100) — Normalized within this report. The top reel = 100, weakest = 0. Use it to find your best-performing reel relative to your own catalog.
Hook score (raw) — watch_s × √reach × (1 + share_pct/100 + save_pct/200). Not a percentage; scales with reach. Higher is better.
Hook rate — watch_s ÷ duration_s × 100. % of the video the average viewer watches. Only shown when duration is available from Instagram.
Watch s — Average watch time per viewer, in seconds (from ig_reels_avg_watch_time).
Replay rate — views ÷ reach. A multiplier (1.2× = 20% of viewers rewatched).
Share % / Save % — Shares or saves as a percentage of reach. Anything above ~2% is unusually strong.
Engagement % — total_interactions ÷ reach × 100. Aggregate "did the audience react?" metric.
Views / Reach / Likes / Comments — Raw counts from Instagram Graph private insights.
